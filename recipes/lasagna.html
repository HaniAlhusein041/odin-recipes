Lasagna Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna recipe features layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella cheese.

It's a comforting Italian dish that's perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 oz) cans tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef, onion, and garlic in a skillet until browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and sugar. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8-10 minutes.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese with egg in a bowl.
  5. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  6. Repeat layers, topping with Parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 minutes.
