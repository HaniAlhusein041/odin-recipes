Spaghetti

Description

Classic spaghetti with a rich and hearty meat sauce, perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions.
  2. Brown ground beef with onion and garlic in a skillet.
  3. Add tomato sauce and seasonings, simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Drain spaghetti and toss with the meat sauce.
  5. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese.
