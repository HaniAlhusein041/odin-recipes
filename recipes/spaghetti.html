Spaghetti Recipe

Description

Classic spaghetti with a rich and hearty meat sauce, perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

This family favorite comes together in under an hour and delivers maximum flavor with minimal effort.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (14 oz) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and cook spaghetti according to package directions.
  2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
  4. Add onion and garlic, cooking until softened (about 5 minutes).
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, and seasonings. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Drain spaghetti and return to the pot.
  7. Add meat sauce to the pasta and toss to combine.
  8. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil garnish.
