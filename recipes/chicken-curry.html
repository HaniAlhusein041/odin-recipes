Chicken Curry Recipe

Description

A flavorful and aromatic chicken curry with tender pieces of chicken in a rich, spiced coconut sauce.

This comforting dish combines warm spices with creamy coconut milk for a perfect balance of flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chicken thighs, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked rice for serving

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
  2. Add onions and cook until soft and translucent (about 5 minutes).
  3. Add garlic, ginger, curry powder, turmeric, and cumin. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add chicken pieces and brown on all sides (about 5 minutes).
  5. Pour in coconut milk and chicken broth, stirring to combine.
  6. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to low, cover, and cook for 25-30 minutes until chicken is tender.
  7. Stir in brown sugar and lime juice. Season with salt to taste.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve over cooked rice.
