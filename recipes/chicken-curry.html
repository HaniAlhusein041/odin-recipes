Chicken Curry

Description

A flavorful and aromatic chicken curry with tender pieces of chicken in a rich, spiced sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chicken thighs, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot and saut√© onions until translucent.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, and curry powder, cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add chicken pieces and brown on all sides.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and bring to a simmer.
  5. Cover and cook for 25-30 minutes until chicken is tender.
  6. Season with salt and garnish with cilantro before serving.
